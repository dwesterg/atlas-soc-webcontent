Software development tools and several example designs are already installed on the SD Card that comes with the Atlas-SoC evaluation board. You can begin immediately by using the Atlas-SoC board as a remote development system via SSH or VNC. For serious development we recommend you download and install the appropriate tool chain on a PC or workstation; details are covered in the "Develop" page.

  • Developing on Atlas-SoC Board (Remote Access)

    There are two methods for accessing the tools and design examples on the Atlas-SoC board:

     

    • Secure Shell (SSH)
      • Connect your the USB cable between your PC and the USB port of the Atlas-SoC board.
      • Open a terminal program on your PC (e.g. name of terminal program).
      • Apply power to the Atlas-SoC board and wait until the HPS User LED begins to flash (heartbeat)
      • Type: ssh root@192.168.7.1

     

    • Virtual Network Computing (VNC)
      • Be sure you have a VNC client application on your PC.
      • Connect your the USB cable between your PC USB port and the USB port of the Atlas-SoC board.
      • Apply power to the Atlas-SoC board and wait until the HPS User LED begins to flash (heartbeat)
      • Launch your VNC client software.
